— The Oruspoon Process

How It Works

Our patented spoon-seal technology ensures every blend is hermetically sealed — untouched from factory to your cup.

01

Fill Your Cup

Take your favourite cup or mug and fill it with 150ml of hot or cold water, depending on your chosen Oruspoon flavor.

02

Peel the Seal

Simply peel back the foil seal on your Oruspoon. The blend has been vacuum-sealed — no human hand has touched it since production.

03

Dip & Stir

Submerge the Oruspoon and stir gently for 10–15 seconds. Watch as the precision-blended powder dissolves into rich, aromatic perfection.

✌

Sip & Savour

Your perfect, authentic beverage is ready. No mess, no measuring, no compromise. Just pure flavor every single time.
